About Us

Youhe bread, founded in 2016, is a new high-end healthy toasted bread chain brand launched by St. Andrew in Wuhan. As a new era baking brand, Youhe bread is well aware of the health needs of contemporary consumers. As the English name uhealth conveys, Youhe bread has never given up its insistence on natural raw materials and crafts. Bake the bread with excellent export feeling, which is delicious and healthy. Adhere to the characteristics of low sugar and low oil to meet every customer's critical taste buds. In the era of fast food, we insist on making every bread slowly and carefully, hoping to present the most quality works to you. Constantly pursuing perfection and refinement, what we care about is not the opinions of others, but the sense of self transcendence, and this spirit is exactly the original intention of our Yuhe bread.
